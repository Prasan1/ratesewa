Listing Requirements

RankSewa lists clinics that meet these standards:

  • Public-facing signage — clearly identifiable as a healthcare facility
  • Clear medical purpose — dedicated to providing medical services
  • Independent access — not hidden inside unrelated businesses
  • Verifiable location — address matches Google Maps / physical presence
  • Accurate representation — no misleading information about services or location
