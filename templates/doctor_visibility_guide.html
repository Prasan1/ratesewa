Grow Your Practice

Tips to rank higher and connect with patients

Your Photo
  • Profiles with photos get 3x more views
  • Use professional headshot with good lighting
  • Genuine smile builds trust
  • Avoid blurry or casual photos
Complete Profile
  • Write 2-3 sentences about your approach
  • List experience and specializations
  • Add workplace for local discovery
  • Complete profiles rank higher
Patient Psychology
  • Patients compare 3-5 profiles
  • They want reassurance, not just skills
  • One good review can be deciding factor
  • Response time signals care
Positive Reviews
  • Thank patients genuinely
  • Keep responses brief and warm
  • Never reveal medical details
  • Avoid overly promotional tone
Negative Reviews
  • Wait 24 hours before responding
  • Acknowledge without being defensive
  • Offer to discuss offline
  • Never argue or blame
Why Bad Reviews Happen
  • Unmet expectations or communication gaps
  • Feeling rushed or dismissed
  • Long wait times without updates
  • Sometimes about staff, not you
