How Does It Work?

Earn Points
  • Write a review: 10 points
  • Detailed review (100+ words): +5 bonus
  • Your review gets marked helpful: 2 points each
  • Doctor responds to your review: 3 points
  • First to review a doctor: +5 bonus
Reviewer Tiers
  • Bronze Basic Contributor: 0-50 points
  • Silver Trusted Reviewer: 51-150 points
  • Gold Expert Reviewer: 151-300 points
  • Platinum Community Leader: 300+ points
