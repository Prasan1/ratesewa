Why We Require Doctor Verification

RankSewa is committed to patient safety. We only allow verified medical professionals on our platform.

  • NMC Registration: We verify your Nepal Medical Council number
  • ID Verification: Government-issued ID confirms your identity
  • Certificate Tracking: We track NMC certificate expiry dates
  • Re-verification: Doctors must re-verify when certificates expire
  • Trust Badge: Verified doctors display a trust badge
  • Clinic Access: Only verified doctors can join clinics
Note: Unverified profiles cannot edit their information, respond to reviews, or join clinic affiliations.
