## Writing Guidelines

### SEO Best Practices:
- **Title:** Include target keyword, keep under 60 characters
- **Meta description:** Include keyword, compelling summary, 150-160 chars
- **Headers:** Use H2, H3 hierarchy with keywords
- **Internal links:** Link to 2-3 other articles and doctor search
- **Images:** At least 1 featured image (1200x630px)
- **Length:** Minimum 1,200 words, ideal 1,800-2,500 words
- **Keywords:** Use target keyword 3-5 times naturally

### Tone & Style:
- **Empathetic:** Acknowledge patient frustrations
- **Practical:** Actionable advice, not just information
- **Honest:** Don't oversell Nepal's healthcare system
- **Empowering:** Position patient as active decision-maker
- **Local:** Use Nepal-specific examples and context

### Call-to-Actions (End of Every Article):
1. "Find [specialty] doctors in [city] on RankSewa"
2. "Review your [specialty] doctor to help other patients"
3. "Share your experience to improve healthcare transparency"
